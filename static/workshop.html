Workflow
1. Set Spread Y — 5 to 10 units is a good start
2. Set Spread X — only if you also want horizontal separation
3. Press Load into Cearum-Web — this saves and activates the spread script
4. Workshop enables recoil + loop automatically
5. Fire a full magazine at a flat wall
6. Take a screenshot of the bullet holes
7. Press Next to go to Step 3 and upload the screenshot
